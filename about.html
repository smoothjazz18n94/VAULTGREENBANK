Our Story

Banking on a better world

Founded in 2008, VaultGreen Bank was born from a simple idea: that everyone — regardless of where they live or how much they earn — deserves access to world-class financial services.

Our Mission

We exist to democratize banking

Too many people are left behind by traditional banking. High fees, opaque processes, and geographic barriers prevent billions from accessing financial services. We're changing that.

Our technology-first approach means we can offer premium banking services at a fraction of the cost of legacy banks — and pass those savings directly to our customers.
